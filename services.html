Shuttle

Ambassador Transportation Corporation's goal is to provide the best ground and airport transportation service on Vancouver Island. All our drivers are professional and have a wealth of experience. Whether you are a first time visitor to the island or returning home, let us pick you up and take you to your destination.

We offer shuttle services from Comox Airport to Comox Valley and surrounding areas, including Nanaimo and Tofino.

Limousine

For all those special occasions: weddings, graduations, showers, anniversaries or just a night out on the town, why not do it in style with our premier limousine service.

Ambassador offers quality stretch limos to Courtenay, Campbell River, Comox Valley, Cumberland, Parksville, Qualicum, and Port Alberni

Tours

Interested in visitng Vancouver Island's unique sites and sampling local drinks and cuisiine. Book one of our tours today and let our experienced drivers and tour guides show you around.

With lots of activities all year round, you don't ever have to miss out. We will arrange to get you and your group there and home safely afterwards.
